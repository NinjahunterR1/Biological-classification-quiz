Physics Question 1:

Find the sum of the infinite series:

1 + 1/2 + 1/4 + 1/8 + 1/16 + ...

Options:

  • A) 1/2
  • B) 1
  • C) 2
  • D) 3/2

Solution:

S = a/(1 - r)
Where:
a = 1
r = 1/2
S = 1/(1 - 1/2) = 2

Physics Question 2:

In the equation P = (a - t^2)/(bx) where P is pressure, x is distance, and time are:

Options:

  • (A) [M¹ L¹ T⁻²]
  • (B) [M¹ L⁰ T⁻²]
  • (C) [M⁻¹ L⁻² T²]
  • (D) [M¹ L⁻² T²]

Solution:

Given:
P = (a - t^2)/(bx)
[p] = [pressure]
[t^2] = [T^2]
Hence,
[b] = [T^2]/([p][x]) = [M T^{-2}]

Chemistry Question 1:

The empirical formula of a compound is CH2O and its vapor density is 30. What is the molecular formula of the compound?

Options:

  • A) C3H6O3
  • B) C2H4O2
  • C) CH2O
  • D) C2H4O

Solution:

Given:
Empirical Formula = CH2O
Empirical Formula Mass = 12 + 2 + 16 = 30
Molar Mass = 2 x V.D. = 2 x 30 = 60
n = Molar Mass / Empirical Formula Mass = 60 / 30 = 2
Molecular Formula = (Empirical Formula)n = (CH2O)2 = C2H4O2.

Chemistry Question 2:

For the gaseous reaction:

Cl2(g) + PCl3(g) → PCl5(g)

If 10 mL of Cl2 completely reacts with PCl3, then the volume of PCl5 formed will be:

Options:

  • A) 10 mL
  • B) 20 mL
  • C) 5 mL
  • D) 1 mL

Solution:

According to the reaction stoichiometry:
1 L Cl2 → 1 L PCl5
Therefore, 10 mL of Cl2 will produce 10 mL of PCl5.

Chemistry Question 3:

12 g of Mg (molar mass = 24 g/mol) reacts completely with HCl to give hydrogen gas. What is the volume at STP?

Options:

  • (a) 22.4 L
  • (b) 11.2 L
  • (c) 44.8 L
  • (d) 6.1 L

Solution:

Mg + 2HCl → MgCl2 + H2
24 g of Mg evolves 22.4 L of H2 at STP.
For 12 g of Mg:
Volume = (22.4 x 12) / 24 = 11.2 L.

Chemistry Question 4:

Given below are two statements, one is labeled as Assertion A and the other is labeled as Reason R.

Assertion A: ClO4 cannot show disproportionation reaction.

Reason R: Minimum oxidation state of Cl in any compound is -1.

Options:

  • (A) A is true and R is false.
  • (B) A is false and R is true.
  • (C) Both A and R are true and R is the correct explanation of A.
  • (D) Both A and R are true but R is NOT the correct explanation of A.

Solution:

Correct Answer: (D)
In ClO4, Cl is present in its highest oxidation state +7, so it cannot show disproportionation reaction.

Botany Question 1:

How many of the following statements are incorrect?

  1. Sodium-potassium pumps move across the cell membrane by passive transport.
  2. The quasi-fluid nature of lipids enables lateral movement of proteins within the lipid bilayer.
  3. Polar solutes require carrier proteins to facilitate their transport across the membrane called facilitated diffusion.
  4. Both facilitated diffusion and active transport are energy-dependent processes.
  5. Neutral solutes may move across the membrane by simple diffusion.

  • (A) One
  • (B) Three
  • (C) Four

  1. Mainly found in mesophyll cells.

  4. Membrane of thylakoid encloses a space called lumen.
